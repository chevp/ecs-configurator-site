Workflow

How It Works

ECS Configurator streamlines the process of creating and editing structured configurations.

1

Open Your .ecscfg Files

Load your existing .ecscfg configuration files directly into the editor. The structured format is parsed and displayed as a navigable entity tree.

2

Edit Entities & Components

Modify entity hierarchies, add or remove components, and adjust property values — all through a visual interface instead of editing raw text files.

3

Preview Assets In-Place

See referenced assets — images, 3D models — rendered directly in the configurator. Validate your configurations visually before using them in your pipeline.
